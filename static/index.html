How this was generated

1,000 app keyword ideas were generated across 40+ domains (health, finance, productivity, food, parenting, pets, home, automotive, mental health, education, social, travel, sports, creative hobbies, real estate, business, niche professional, seniors, sustainability, dating, weather, legal, faith, trades, outdoor adventure, music, cooking, student life, elderly care, pregnancy, financial planning, home improvement, fashion, language, games, events, transportation, wellness, writing, community, science, and more).

Each keyword was searched on the iTunes Search API (US App Store, top 10 results) and scored using the same Demand vs Supply framework: Demand = total ratings across the top 10 apps, Supply = average rating count of the top 10 incumbents. The gap between demand rank and supply rank determines the opportunity verdict.

A composite score (0–110) adds bonuses for stale competitors (>3 not updated in a year), low-rated incumbents (>3 below 3.5 stars), weak average ratings (<1K), high demand level, low market concentration (Gini <0.4), and a high ratio of weak apps in the top 10.
